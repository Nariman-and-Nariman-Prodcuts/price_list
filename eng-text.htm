Ordinary powders only contain 10-15% detergents, the remaining contents are substances that have nothing to do with removing dirt such as phosphates, fillers, fluorescents brightening agents. Crux is a 100% pure detergent.

Why are phosphates which are to be used as fertilizers being mixed in ordinary washing powders?

Phosphates are mixed in ordinary washing powders up to 50% to increase their weight and soften hard water. Phosphates have nothing to do with removing dirt.
